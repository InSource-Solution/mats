Our Mission

At Mats Towing & Transport, our objective is to offer commuters in the UAE and the GCC top-notch, dependable, and stress-free vehicle recovery and roadside assistance services. We endeavor to:

Provide unparalleled repair and towing service that prioritizes the safety and satisfaction of our clients. Establish enduring relationships with our consumers by consistently surpassing their expectations.

We must adapt and innovate our services to accommodate the evolving requirements of our consumers and the industry.

Maintain the principles of transparency, integrity, and honesty in all facets of our business.

Our Vision

Our aim is to establish ourselves as the premier provider of vehicle recovery services and roadside assistance in the United Arab Emirates and the broader Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC). Our objective is to:

Establish the industry’s benchmark for excellence by providing unparalleled customer service and quality.

Encourage an environment of perpetual development, in which innovation is the driving force behind our success and enables us to maintain our position at the vanguard of the industry.

Ensure that we are the preferred option for vehicles in need of assistance, regardless of their location, by broadening our reach and capabilities.
